Distribution of Tokens

  • 25% of STAR Tokens will be sold to Public Contributors
  • 20% of STAR Tokens are reserved as incentives for Researchers
  • 20% of STAR Tokens are reserved as incentives to build world-wide Communities
  • 10% of STAR Tokens are reserved for Company Expenses
  • 25% of STAR Tokens are reserved for TEAM, Advisors, and Early Backers. Team is allocated tokens with 6-months cliff and 4 year vesting period.

Allocation of Funds

  • 65% of the funds will be used for R&D and platform development
  • 15% of the funds will be used for Marketing and building World-wide Communities
  • 10% of the funds will be used for Administration and day-to-day smooth running of operations
  • 5% of the funds will be reserved for legal expenses
  • 5% of the funds will be reserved for any contingency

Instructions for Participating in Astra Token Sale

  • A secure online account will be opened for the community members who register for the Astra Token Sale using the Whitelist Link. The community members who complete the online registration will earn 5 Free STAR Tokens.
  • Only verified or whitelisted community members will be able to participate in the Astra Token Sale. Please register as soon as possible so that we can timely complete the required KYC process.
  • Astra’s official Ethereum Address for sending your contributions will be posted only on your personal account page. It will not be posted anywhere else. Please ensure that you are at https://www.goastra.info website.
  • Astra Tokens will be delivered only to those accounts which pass the KYC process. The ETH contributions of accounts that do not pass the KYC process will be returned.
  • The US community members will only see the token purchase link in their accounts when they tick the Checkbox affirming that they are an “Accredited Investor”.
  • Please follow these instructions for sending Ethers and purchasing Astra Tokens.
